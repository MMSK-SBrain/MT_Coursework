## Design Guidelines

### Color Palette (EQ LIFE Branding)
- **Primary:** Deep Teal (#0B6E6E) — anchoring, professional, calming
- **Secondary:** Warm Coral (#E8735A) — energy, emotion, warmth
- **Accent 1:** Soft Gold (#D4A843) — insight, value, achievement
- **Accent 2:** Slate Blue (#4A6B8A) — trust, depth, knowledge
- **Neutral Base:** Off-white (#F7F5F0) background, Charcoal (#2D2D2D) text
- **Highlight:** Gentle Violet (#8B6FAE) — reflection, transformation

### Module Color-Coding (Accent Strip / Tab on Each Slide)
| Module | Accent Color | Hex |
|---|---|---|
| Opening & Closing | Deep Teal | #0B6E6E |
| Module 1: EI Essentials | Warm Coral | #E8735A |

| Module 6: Inclusive Action | Soft Gold | #D4A843 |

### Typography
- **Headings:** Montserrat Bold (or Raleway Bold) — clean, modern, confident
- **Body Text:** Open Sans Regular — highly readable at all sizes
- **Accent / Quotes:** Playfair Display Italic — for definitions and pull-quotes
- **Minimum font size:** 24pt for body text on slides, 32pt+ for key phrases

### General Design Rules
- Maximum 6 lines of text per slide — key phrases only, not paragraphs
- Visual-heavy: every slide should have an image, icon, diagram, or graphic element
- White space is a design element — do not fill every corner
- Consistent module color tab/strip on left edge or top edge of each slide
- Slide numbers in bottom-right corner, small and subtle
- Disha and Reynlab logos in footer area — small, unobtrusive
- Use high-quality stock photography featuring diverse South Asian professionals where possible

### Special Slide Type Conventions
- **Movie clip slides:** Show only "Let's Watch..." with a play icon. No clip title displayed (IP protection for screen visibility).
- **Discussion question slides:** Questions in large, readable font (28pt+), numbered. Warm background color.
- **Case study slides:** Full scenario text on slide since participants do not have it in their handbook. Use a slightly smaller font (22-24pt) with ample line spacing.
- **Activity instruction slides:** Clear step-by-step numbered instructions. Icon for each step.
- **EQ Prism slides:** QR code placeholder prominent and centered. Clear 1-2-3 instructions below.
