# Course Requirements: Emotional Intelligence & Microaggression Awareness

**Created:** 2026-03-20
**Status:** Structuring
**Initiative:** Disha (C3 Women Employees Program)
**Requestor:** Mano
**Facilitator:** Sandhya
**Scheduled Date:** 7 April 2026

## 1. Course Context
- **Topic:** Emotional Intelligence, Bias, Stereotypes & Microaggression Awareness
- **Industry/Domain:** Corporate / Leadership Development
- **Duration:** 1 day (6 hours net training)
- **Schedule:** 9:30 AM - 5:15 PM (45 min lunch + two 15-min tea breaks)
- **Description:** A one-day experiential learning program under the Disha initiative for C3 women employees, covering emotional intelligence foundations, unconscious bias, stereotypes, and microaggressions — with a focus on recognition, response, and building inclusive workspaces.

## 2. Target Audience
- **Primary Learners:** C3-level women employees (Disha initiative)
- **Experience Level:** 5+ years professional experience
- **Roles:** Mix of individual contributors and team leads/managers
- **Group Size:** 18-20 participants
- **Prerequisites:** None — no prior EI or DEI training
- **Job Role:** Various roles across the organization

## 3. Learning Outcomes
### Course Outcomes (COs):
1. **CO-1:** Recognize personal emotional patterns and stress triggers through self-reflection exercises
2. **CO-2:** Apply emotional regulation strategies to manage responses in workplace situations
3. **CO-3:** Identify microaggressions and subtle biases in everyday workplace interactions
4. **CO-4:** Respond to microaggressions constructively using assertive communication techniques
5. **CO-5:** Develop a personal action plan for fostering inclusive behavior in their teams

### Three Pillars (Equal Weight):
- Emotional Intelligence (Goleman's 4-Quadrant Model)
- Bias & Stereotypes
- Microaggressions (recognition + response)

## 4. Content Structure
### Modules (6):
1. Emotional Intelligence Essentials (50 min)
2. Understanding Bias — The Invisible Lens (55 min)
3. Stereotypes — Scripts We Inherit (45 min)
4. Recognizing Microaggressions (55 min)
5. Responding & Intervening (55 min)
6. From Awareness to Inclusive Action (50 min)

### Delivery Approach:
- Case study driven (real scenarios + movie clips)
- Hollywood + Bollywood movie clips (3 options per slot, all in instructor manual)
- Questions shown on slides, participants write answers in handbook
- Experiential and discussion-heavy
- ~30% theory / 70% experiential activities

### EI Framework:
- Goleman's 4-Quadrant Model (Self-Awareness, Self-Management, Social Awareness, Relationship Management)

### Bias Framework:
- Types: unconscious, affinity, confirmation, attribution bias

### Response Framework:
- 3D Model: Direct, Distract, Delegate

## 5. Materials
### Instructor Manual:
- All movie clip options (3 per slot) with facilitation guidance
- Full case study scenarios with discussion guides
- Slide-by-slide guidance ("Show X, say Y")
- Answer keys and expected responses
- Facilitation notes and timing

### Participant Handbook:
- Session summaries and key concepts
- Blank journaling / notes space
- "Case Study Discussion" placeholders (no full scenarios)
- Blank answer spaces (questions on slides only)
- Personal action plan template
- Confidentiality notice on cover
- "For use during facilitated sessions only" footer
- NO proprietary content — workbook is incomplete without facilitator
