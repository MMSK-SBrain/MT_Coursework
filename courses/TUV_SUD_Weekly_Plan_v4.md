# TÜV SÜD Automotive Service Technician — Weekly Plan v4

**Program:** 8 weeks mandatory (120 hrs) + 3 weeks optional extension (45 hrs)
**Format:** 5 days/week × 3 hrs/day = 15 hrs/week
**Design Principle:** Every week tells a story. Every day earns the right to teach the next.

---

## Design Philosophy — The Story Arc

A learner with zero automotive background walks in on Day 1. The program must earn their trust and build their confidence in a specific sequence:

1. **First, show them the whole car.** Before safety rules make sense, before a micrometer matters, the learner needs to *see* what they're going to work on. Name the systems. Open the bonnet. Get under the car on a lift. Point at things.
2. **Then, teach them to stay alive.** Safety has *context* now — "this is why HV gloves matter" lands differently when you've just seen a hybrid battery pack.
3. **Then, give them the language.** Electrical theory, wiring diagrams, ECU architecture — the *grammar* of every system they'll study from Week 3 onwards.
4. **Then, system by system, spiral deeper.** Each week opens with a relatable "big picture" before any deep dive — air and fuel before injector types, vehicle dynamics before suspension geometry, power flow before gear ratios. The learner always knows *why* before learning *how*.
5. **Finally, integrate everything.** The real technician doesn't think in chapters.
