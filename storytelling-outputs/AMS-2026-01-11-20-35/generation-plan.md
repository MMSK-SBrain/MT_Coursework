## Generation Strategy

### Grouping Decisions
- **Days with 3 SOs on related topic:** Group into one cohesive story
- **Days with mixed Knowledge + Skill:** Group if supporting each other
- **Safety-critical topics:** Emphasize through Cautionary Tale technique
- **Complex technical topics:** Use Teacher-Led narrative (first-person instructor)
- **First day of each week:** Use Teacher-Led to establish presence

### Technique Rotation
- **Avoid consecutive repetition:** No same technique 2 days in a row
- **Variety per week:** Aim for 4-5 different techniques per week
- **Teacher-Led usage:** ~7-8 days across 30 days (23-27%)
- **Balance:** Mix of narrative-driven and problem-framing approaches
